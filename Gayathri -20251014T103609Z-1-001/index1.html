Our Dance Programs

Structured learning paths from foundation to mastery

Bharatanatyam

The classical dance of Tamil Nadu, known for its grace, purity, and spiritual expression

Beginner Level

Foundation of Bharatanatyam - Basic positions and movements

  • Basic Aramandi position
  • Fundamental Adavus (8 types)
  • Hand gestures (Mudras)
  • Simple Sollukattus
  • Namaskar sequence

Intermediate Level

Building technique and introducing choreography

  • Complex Adavu combinations
  • Jathiswaram choreography
  • Basic Abhinaya (expressions)
  • Rhythm patterns (Tala)
  • Short dance sequences

Advanced Level

Mastering expressions and complete performances

  • Varnam performance
  • Advanced Abhinaya
  • Padam interpretation
  • Stage presence
  • Complete repertoire

Kuchipudi

The dynamic dance-drama from Andhra Pradesh, blending pure dance with theatrical storytelling

Beginner Level

Basic positions, steps and theatrical elements

  • Mandala sthanakas
  • Basic Adavus
  • Simple Mudras
  • Tarangam basics
  • Entry sequences

Intermediate Level

Dramatic expressions and traditional items

  • Sabdam performance
  • Character portrayal
  • Rhythmic variations
  • Basic storytelling
  • Jatiswaram pieces

Advanced Level

Complete dance-dramas and solo performances

  • Full Tarangam with pot
  • Padam interpretations
  • Character roles
  • Advanced Abhinaya
  • Traditional repertoire

Mohiniyattam

The enchanting dance of Kerala, embodying feminine grace and subtle expressions

Beginner Level

Graceful movements and basic postures

  • Aramandi variations
  • Basic Adavus
  • Gentle hand movements
  • Swaying motions
  • Simple Mudras

Intermediate Level

Lyrical sequences and subtle expressions

  • Cholkettu patterns
  • Gentle Abhinaya
  • Flowing sequences
  • Traditional songs
  • Graceful transitions

Advanced Level

Divine feminine expressions and classical pieces

  • Varnam performances
  • Classical Padam
  • Subtle expressions
  • Traditional repertoire
  • Spiritual themes
